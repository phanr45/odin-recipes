Shin Ramen

This shin ramen recipe is a fantastic midnight hunger fix.

I highly recommend you eat this at midnight or later, it just taste infitely better !

Ingredients

ingredients are very simple

  • One pack of Shin Ramen
  • 500 Ml of Water
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Instructions

  1. Add 500 ML of water to the pot
  2. Add seasoning and veggie packets to the pot
  3. Once the water boils add the noodles
  4. In the middle of cooking add in 2 eggs
  5. turn off the stove
  6. place the cheese on top
